Weekly Workout Plan

4-day split adapted to your cycle phase

Day 1: Lower Body Strength + Reformer

Follicular: Heavy | Luteal: Moderate
  • 🦵 Goblet Squats - 3x10-12
  • 🍑 Romanian Deadlifts - 3x10-12
  • 🔥 Hip Thrusts - 3x12-15
  • 🦵 Walking Lunges - 3x10/leg
  • 🧘 Reformer Pilates - 45 min

Day 2: Upper Body Push + Core

Follicular: Heavy | Luteal: Moderate
  • 💪 Push-ups (or DB Press) - 3x8-12
  • 🎯 Overhead Press - 3x10-12
  • ✨ Lateral Raises - 3x12-15
  • 🔥 Tricep Dips - 3x10-12
  • 🎯 Plank - 3x30-60 sec
  • 🧘 Dead Bug - 3x10/side

Day 3: Lower Body Power + HIIT

Follicular: HIIT OK | Luteal: Reduce intensity
  • ⚡ Jump Squats - 3x8
  • 🍑 Bulgarian Split Squats - 3x8/leg
  • 🔥 Kettlebell Swings - 3x15
  • 💨 HIIT: 20 sec on/10 off x 8 rounds
  • 🧘 Hip Flexor Stretch - 2 min/side

Day 4: Upper Body Pull + Core

Follicular: Heavy | Luteal: Moderate
  • 💪 Rows (DB or Band) - 3x10-12
  • 🎯 Lat Pulldowns or Pull-ups - 3x6-10
  • ✨ Face Pulls - 3x15
  • 🔥 Bicep Curls - 3x12-15
  • 🎯 Russian Twists - 3x20
  • 🧘 Bird Dog - 3x10/side

🔄 Cycle Adaptations

Follicular (Days 1-14): Lift heavy, progressive overload, HIIT is fine. Higher carb tolerance.

Luteal (Days 15-28): Moderate weights, focus on form, reduce HIIT intensity. Increase carbs and magnesium.
